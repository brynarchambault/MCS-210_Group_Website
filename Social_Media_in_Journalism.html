History of Social Media

The history of social media starts with the emergence of the internet and personal computers in the 1970-80s, followed by blogging and online bulletin boards in the 90s. The first social media platform, Six Degrees, was created in 1997. Friendster and LinkedIn were released in 2002 with different features. MySpace and Facebook, 2003 and 2004 respectively, both became extremely popular among young adults. More recent social media platforms include Twitter (X), Instagram, and Snapchat. Each social media platform varies in some way. This evolution will continue, especially since social media is relatively new.
